About Us

THE JATAYU LEGACY

Team Jatayu represents the pinnacle of student motorsport engineering. Named after the legendary eagle from ancient mythology, we embody strength, speed, and unwavering determination.

Founded with a vision to create world-class racing vehicles, our team brings together the brightest engineering minds, united by a shared passion for motorsport excellence. Every component we design, every system we optimize, reflects our commitment to pushing the boundaries of what's possible.

Our approach combines rigorous engineering methodology with creative innovation. We don't just build vehicles—we engineer solutions that challenge conventions and set new standards in student motorsport.
